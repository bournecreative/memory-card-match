How To Play

Espionage, is an exercise/game to test your field agent wits and memory to properly identify cards with matching agent identities.

  • There are 18 cards with 9 matching pairs, randomly assorted.
  • Each card shields an covert agent's identity.
  • Player can flip 2 cards per attempt.
  • Match all 9 agents to WIN!

Good luck field agent.
