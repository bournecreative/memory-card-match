How To Play

Espionage, is an exercise/game to test your field agent wits and memory to properly identify matching agent identities.

  • There are 18 cards with 9 matching pairs, randomly assorted on the gameboard
  • Each card shields a covert agent's identity
  • Each turn allows the player to reveal 2 cards
  • Player must match all 9 agents within 2 minutes to Win!

Good luck field agent.
